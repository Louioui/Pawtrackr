# Pawtrackr

The all-in-one app for pet grooming and service professionals.

Pawtrackr helps pet groomers and service providers manage their clients, track sessions in real-time, process payments, and analyze business performance.

## Features

### Client & Pet Management
- **Client Profiles** - Store client contact info, addresses, and notes
- **Pet Records** - Track breed, weight, health notes, behavior tags, and grooming preferences
- **Emergency Contacts** - Keep emergency contact information for each client
- **Photo Storage** - Save pet photos for easy identification

### Session Tracking
- **Check-In/Check-Out** - Start and complete service sessions with one tap
- **Live Session Timer** - Real-time duration tracking for active visits
- **Before & After Photos** - Document your work with photo comparisons
- **Behavior Notes** - Record pet behavior during each session

### Services & Payments
- **Custom Service Catalog** - Create and manage your services with pricing
- **Service Categories** - Organize by Grooming, Add-ons, Special Care, and Packages
- **Multiple Payment Methods** - Accept Cash, Credit, Debit, and Zelle
- **Transaction Tracking** - Record payment references and notes

### Business Insights
- **Revenue Dashboard** - View daily, weekly, and monthly earnings
- **Performance Charts** - Track revenue trends with interactive graphs
- **Top Services** - See which services generate the most revenue
- **Custom Date Ranges** - Filter analytics by any time period

### Security
- **PIN Lock** - Protect your business data with a 4-digit PIN
- **Biometric Authentication** - Unlock with Face ID or Touch ID

### Data & Export
- **Visit History** - Complete timeline of all pet visits
- **CSV Export** - Export visit records for bookkeeping
- **Local Storage** - All data stays on your device
