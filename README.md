# Pawtrackr

Track your pet's health and happiness.

Pawtrackr is an iOS app designed to help pet owners keep track of their furry friends' health, activities, and important milestones.

## Features

- **Pet Profiles** - Create detailed profiles for each of your pets
- **Health Tracking** - Log vet visits, medications, and vaccinations
- **Activity Monitoring** - Track walks, feeding times, and daily activities
- **Reminders** - Never miss a vet appointment or medication schedule
- **Insights** - View trends and patterns in your pet's health data

## Requirements

- iOS 15.0+
- iPhone and iPad

## Installation

Pawtrackr is available on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/pawtrackr).
